2010 Results

Detroit Titan Invite

 Men finish 4th of 7 in collegiate varsity race
Meet: Running Fit/Detroit Titan Invite
Date: September 11, 2010
Site: Cass Benton Park; Northville, MI
Results: Men 4th of 7, Women nts

Men’s Results
Women’s Results

On September 11th, the Michigan Running Club attended its first meet of the fall cross country season, the varsity collegiate Detroit Titan Invite.  12 men and 2 women attended the meet, competing with several NCAA teams and several community college teams.

Mollie Pozolo, who ran 19:58 on a hilly 5k course for 15th overall, led the women’s contingent.  After her was Lindsay Doherty, who finished 32nd overall with a time of 21:45.

The men finished 4th in the collegiate 4-mile race on the challenging Cass Benton course.  The men had 125 points, behind Eastern Michigan (41), Wayne State (48), and U of D Mercy (56).  Nathan Peters was the first MRunner across the line at 21:23, good for 21st place.  Max Hock (21:31) was right on his heels in 23rd place, while Joe Billian made his MRun debut with a 26th place finish at 21:37.  Rounding out the the scoring were Jimmy Adams (42nd place, 22:27) and Jason Mahakian (44th place, 22:29).

A lucky few without Friday class will attend the Spartan Invitational in East Lansing, MI on September 17, before opening their NIRCA cross country campaign on September 26th with the Michigan Fall Classic.
